== Installation ==
WordPress versions: 4.4.0 and up.

Step-by-step Guide:

- <strong>Step 1:</strong> Navigate to your WordPress Plugin Directory to install tolktalkCX Contact Widget plugin and activate it on your WordPress website.([Read More](https://wordpress.org/support/article/managing-plugins/#automatic-plugin-installation));
<p></p>
- <strong>Step 2:</strong> Go to Settings > tolktalkCX Contact Widget > Connect with tolktalkCX;
<p></p>
- <strong>Step 3:</strong> Add login credentials to get tolktalkCX Token;
<p></p>
- <strong>Step 4:</strong> Login/SignUp to your tolktalkCX account ([tolktalkCX.com](https://dashboard.tolktalk.com/login));
<p></p>
- <strong>Step 5:</strong> Go to Website Widgets > tolktalkCX Contact Widget;
<p></p>
- <strong>Step 6:</strong> Customize and Save the tolktalkCX Contact Widget to fit your website style and design;
<p></p>
- <strong>Step 7:</strong> Navigate back to WordPress Admin Panel and go to Appearance > Widgets; and 
<p></p>
- <strong>Step 8:</strong> Finally display your tolktalkCX Contact Widget on your website by selecting the tolktalkCX Contact Widget from your Available Widgets and adding it to the Footer of your website.
